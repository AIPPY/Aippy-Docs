When your web application is complete, you can **launch it** and make it available to users worldwide. Additionally, you have the flexibility to **share a development version** to collect feedback before the official release. Aippy streamlines the process of publishing, updating, and distributing your applications with minimal effort.

<Tip>
  Your deployed application will be accessible to anyone with the URL. Ensure
  you're prepared for public access before publishing\!
</Tip>

## Launching your application

When your project is complete, **publish it to create a public URL.** This enables you to showcase your application or collaborate with team members and stakeholders effectively.

### Publishing Your Application

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Navigate to the **editor view** and click the **Publish** button (globe icon) in the top right corner.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    A configuration window will appear with options for **deploying your application**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Select the **Publish** button to initiate the deployment. The button will display **Publishing** during this process.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    Upon successful deployment, the button will change to **Update** for future modifications.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    Your application is now **live** and accessible through the provided URL.

    <Note>
      1. The deployment process usually completes within a minute. You'll receive a
         notification upon completion.
      2. In the project settings, you can choose to **display or hide the Aippy badge** on your deployed application. Configure this setting before publishing.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6">
    <Info>
      **Leverage preview mode for iterative development**

      - Preview mode is an invaluable tool that enables you and your team to review the latest changes without impacting the production version of your application.
      - Many users utilize this feature for iterative development, real-time feedback collection, and collaborative testing before final deployment.
    </Info>
    Preview mode allows you to **review your latest modifications** without affecting the production version.

    To access preview mode:

    1. Open the **Publish** dialog.
    2. Find the **Preview** section.
    3. Click the generated **preview URL** to view or share your development updates.

    The preview URL is generated instantly and can be shared with team members for review.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 7">
    To modify your project's subdomain or name:

    1. Access **Settings**.
    2. Update the project name in the designated section.
    3. Click **Rename Project**.
    4. Remember to **republish** to update the subdomain and make changes publicly visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 8">
    For custom domain configuration, [follow these instructions](https://docs.aippy.ai/tips-tricks/custom-domain).
  </Step>
</Steps>

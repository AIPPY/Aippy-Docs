## Current Features

- **AI-Powered App Generation**: Transform your ideas into fully functional frontend applications using natural language prompts.
- **Real-Time Development**: Experience instant preview with comprehensive version history and seamless rollback capabilities.
- **Smart Version Control**: Automated checkpoints and intuitive version management for worry-free experimentation.
- **One-Click Deployment**: Launch your projects instantly with zero configuration.
- **Community Showcase**: Explore inspiring projects from the Aippy community and share your own creations with the world.

- **Visual Development Tools**: Edit and customize your UI in real-time with our intuitive visual editor.
- **Advanced Deployment**: Enhance your projects with custom domains, payment processing, and seamless third-party integrations.
